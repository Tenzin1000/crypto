About
Digital Counsulting
We are 3M Global Holdings LLC, an international products transaction tracking and gold trading company registered in the US and doing business out of the UAE. We offer interested parties an opportunity to participate in and benefit from our gold purchasing and sales operations. We procure gold on a transaction basis from Africa. The refined gold is purchased from gold mining representatives in kilogram bars at a significant discount below World Market Pricing (WMP). The gold is then transferred to our UAE office where it is sold for a profit to our Dubai buyers. This business operation and opportunity is the result of many years of establishing the proper relationships with numerous gold mining sales representatives and gold buyers in Dubai. We conduct multiple transactions per month.
What We Do
We have established Financial Participation Programs (FPPs) that provides Annual Percentage Yeild (APY) of twenty percentage (20%) of the principal funding for all participants in an FPP via the following terms:
Every FPP funding has acapacity limit of $1,000,000 USD and closes when funding reaches maximum funding.
  • The participants cannot withdraw their Participation funds from FPP for six months.
A participant can place funding in as many FPPs as they desire.
  • The participants can withdraw their APY at anytime after the APY is placed in their accounts, no need less than monthly.
An FPP account will be established for each participant.
  • The participants cannot use their APY funds in a specific FPP to re-participate in that FPP.
